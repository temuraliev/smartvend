Slide 2

Problem

Vending machines must decide when to dispense an item, return money, or show an error.

Our system uses basic digital logic to make this decision clearly and correctly.

Slide 3

System Overview

Inputs

  • Coin 1 inserted
  • Coin 2 inserted
  • Product selected
  • Product available
  • Cancel button

Outputs

  • Dispense item
  • Return money
  • Show error
Slide 4

Logic Design

The product price is two coins.

P = C1 . C2
M = C1 + C2

D = X' . S . A . P
R = M . (X + S . A')
E = X' . [(S . A . P') + (S . A') + (S' . M)]
